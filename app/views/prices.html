Pricing and online purchase policy

Timetabled class passes

  • All timetabled class pass sessions bought online, never expire and are valid until used. There is no time limit.
  • Passes can be used for any class with the same, or shorter, session length. They are not tied to a specific class.
  • You may also buy passes to give away to your friends or family (ask at the studio, to have passes transfered to someone else).
  • If pass prices go up in the future, we will continue to accept passes purchased previously at the lower price.

Concessions

We offer discounted rates on the evening classes for:

  • Students
  • Seniors
  • Services Personnel (past or present)
  • NHS Staff & Care Workers

On your first visit, we may ask to see ID showing your entitlement and make a note on your account. On subsequent visits, there is no need to show ID. Just select the discount passes in the payment form.

If you need a concession for reasons not listed here, just ask. All requests will receive due consideration.

There is also a Monday Community Class, where admission is donation based. You may give whatever you can afford, attend free of charge if you need to, or make a contribution that isn't money, like making a "pay it forward" donation of your time to a charitable cause or doing something selfless and heroic for someone who needs you.

Freestyle

Freestyle passes are used for personal practice time at the loft. They cover a pre-booked, pre-paid, personal practice session of up to 2 hours without an instructor present. We also use them for some special-offer classes, Acroyoga and Community class (which is donation based).
